THE METHODOLOGY

Six paths. Every trade.
No shortcuts. Ever.

Sage runs all 6 simultaneously — not sequentially, not when it feels like it. Every single analysis applies all 6 paths and shows you exactly which ones confirmed the trade.

PATH 01
📈
Price Action
Al Brooks + Jesse Livermore methodology. Every candle tells a story. Bull bars = buyers winning. Rejection wicks. Failed breakouts trapping retail before the real move.
Example: "A 2-legged pullback on low volume formed at EMA 21. This is the classic Jesse Livermore re-entry setup — trend resumes after retail stops are cleared."
PATH 02
🏦
Smart Money / ICT
Unmitigated Order Blocks, Breaker Blocks, Fair Value Gaps, Inversion FVGs, BSL/SSL liquidity, Premium/Discount zones, CHoCH/BOS, Power of 3 cycle.
Example: "Price swept the SSL at 1.15020 with a long wick and immediately reversed — that liquidity sweep into the discount zone is the ICT entry signal."
PATH 03
📊
Technical Indicators
EMA 8/21/50/200 stack, RSI momentum state, ADX trend strength, ATR volatility. Applied with context — not blindly. ADX below 20 = range strategy only.
Example: "RSI 39 in a downtrend — not oversold, still room to fall. ADX 28 confirms trend. Do NOT fade this — wait for it to reach the OTE zone."
PATH 04
🌀
Wyckoff Market Cycle
Accumulation → Markup → Distribution → Markdown. Sage identifies the current phase and tells you what comes next. The Spring setup is the best Wyckoff entry.
Example: "This is a Wyckoff Spring — false breakdown below support with a rapid recovery. Volume spiked on the low. Institutional accumulation confirmed."
PATH 05
🗺
Multi-Timeframe
Daily → 4H → 1H top-down analysis. At least 3 timeframes must agree before a trade is issued. A 1H signal against the Daily trend gets rejected automatically.
Example: "Daily is bearish. 4H is bearish. 1H just formed an OB rejection. All 3 timeframes aligned short — this is the highest-probability setup of the week."
PATH 06
📰
Fundamentals & Economics
Live economic calendar. DXY direction. Risk-on/off environment. Central bank sentiment. NFP/CPI/FOMC — if it's coming, Sage says WAIT before a trade fires.
Example: "NFP in 90 minutes. I'm not giving you a trade right now. This release can move EUR/USD 150+ pips in seconds. We wait, then trade the reaction."
